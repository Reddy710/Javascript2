About Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was a renowned Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India's space and missile development programs and is often referred to as the "Missile Man of India." Dr. Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, and passed away on July 27, 2015.

Legacy

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam's contributions to science, technology, and education are immense. He was a source of inspiration for millions of people, especially the youth of India. His vision for India's development and his dedication to education continue to influence generations.
